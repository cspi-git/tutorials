Name: How to take down any abused IP?
Tags: security, safety, take-down, abused

1. Make sure you have the IP.
2. Visit https://app.netlas.io/host/ and search the IP
3. Find **Abuse:** then report the IP via email.
4. Wait for them to take action and take care of it(Depends If the IP has been abused for like DDoSing, botnet & etc).
